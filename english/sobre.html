Story

The foundation in 1932 occurred at the time of the economic discovery of the interior of Paraná. THE Pelho family, a traditional local, has invested all its savings in this new initiative, revolutionary for the time. The Founder Eduardo Simões Pelho, dendowed with particular vision has guided the company's business for more than 50 years, many of them of your son E. S. Pelho Filho, current CEO. The name of the company is inspired by the name of the family.

The company's growth was virtually instantaneous. In the first 5 years, it has served 18 countries. Hit the mark of 100 countries in just 15 years of existence. To date, it has already provided 740 million of different users in billions of different orders.

The growth in the number of employees is also astounding. Today, it is the largest employer in Brazil, but even after only 5 years of its existence, it already had 30 thousand employees. Out of Brazil, there are 240 thousand employees, in addition to the 890 thousand Brazilians in the Jacarezinho offices around the country.

Given the economic importance of the company to Brazil, the Pelho family has already received several awards, honors and decorations. All the presidents of Brazil have already visited the facilities of Mirror Fashion, as well as presidents of the European Union, Asia and the Secretary-General of the UN.
